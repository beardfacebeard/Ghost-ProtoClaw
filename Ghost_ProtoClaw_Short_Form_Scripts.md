# POSTING STRATEGY

## Week 1: Awareness
- Post Angle 1 (OpenClaw Rescue), Angle 2 (YouTube Dream), Angle 5 (Deploy Flex)
- Goal: get in front of people already in the AI agent conversation

## Week 2: Desire
- Post Angle 3 (Quit Your Job), Angle 6 (Price Shock), Angle 7 (Behind The Scenes)
- Goal: make them want what you have

## Week 3: Decision
- Post Angle 8 (Stop Typing Prompts), Angle 9 (Window Closing), Angle 12 (What I Built)
- Goal: push fence-sitters over the edge

## Week 4+: Breadth
- Post Angle 4 (13 Agents), Angle 10 (Existing Business), Angle 11 (Templates/Custom)
- Goal: expand to adjacent audiences

## Posting Frequency
- TikTok: 2 videos per day (1 new angle + 1 repost with different hook)
- YouTube Shorts: 1 video per day
- Instagram Reels: 1 video per day
- Rotate the same videos across platforms — you'll only look fresh to new viewers

## Hook Variations
For each angle, record 3-4 different hook variations. Example for Angle 1:
- "You tried OpenClaw didn't you 😬"
- "OpenClaw kicked my ass too"
- "If you gave up on OpenClaw, watch this"
- "OpenClaw is amazing — for developers. If you're not one, watch this."

Test which hook pulls the most views and double down.

# SHOT LIST FOR A SINGLE RECORDING SESSION

If you want to knock out ALL 12 short-form videos in one session, here's the order:

## Face Cam Clips (Record all in one sitting — 30-45 min)
Record each line 3-4 times. Plan on re-recording the ones that need more energy.

1. All "hook" opening lines (30 seconds of face cam per video = 6 minutes)
2. All "middle content" face cam lines (30 seconds per video)
3. All "CTA close" face cam lines (10 seconds per video)

## Screen Recording Sessions (30-45 min)
Record these once, clip them for every video:

1. **The Full Deploy** — one long recording of the Railway deploy, login, dashboard landing
2. **Ghost Operator Tour** — scroll through all 5 agents, show the chat, show a workflow
3. **TikTok Shop Operator Tour** — scroll through all 10 agents
4. **Template Selection** — show the 13 templates, click into a few
5. **Template Editor** — show editing prompts, workflows, knowledge
6. **Activity Log** — show agent activity over time
7. **Costs Dashboard** — show revenue/costs tracking

## B-Roll Ideas (Stock or Self-Recorded)
- Frustrated person at laptop
- Code on screen / error messages
- Morning coffee routine
- Checking phone in bed
- Walking/lifestyle shots
- Stack of cash or revenue charts (aspirational but not tacky)

## CapCut Template Approach
Create a master CapCut template with:
- Bottom-third caption placeholder
- Top hook caption placeholder
- Your intro/outro animation
- Background music selection
- Brand colors for text overlays

Then clone it 12 times and fill in each video. This cuts edit time by 70%.
