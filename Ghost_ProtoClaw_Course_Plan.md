# Ghost ProtoClaw Mastery Course — Complete Plan

## Course Overview

**Course Name:** Ghost ProtoClaw Mastery: From Deploy to Autopilot
**Price:** $49 (add-on) / standalone
**Target Audience:** Non-technical business owners who bought the App Only tier ($149) and need guided setup help, OR standalone buyers who want a structured walkthrough before purchasing.
**Format:** Written step-by-step guides + video lesson scripts (for recording walkthroughs)
**Estimated Length:** 6 modules, ~25 lessons total
**Delivery:** Skool, Teachable, or bundled as downloadable content

---

## Course Philosophy

This isn't a "watch me code" dev course. It's a "follow these steps and your AI team is live" operator course. Every lesson ends with the student having something working. No fluff, no filler — just the steps, the why behind them, and the result.

## Module Breakdown

### MODULE 1: Welcome & What You're Working With (3 Lessons)

**Goal:** Student understands what Ghost ProtoClaw actually does, what they need before starting, and picks the right template for their business.

**Lesson 1.1 — What Ghost ProtoClaw Actually Is (And Isn't)**
- It's not a chatbot — it's a full AI operations team
- 13 pre-built business templates, each with specialized agents
- How agents, workflows, and knowledge bases work together
- The difference between Ghost ProtoClaw and just "using ChatGPT"
- *Deliverable: Student understands the product and can explain it simply*
- *Video: 5-7 min overview with dashboard walkthrough*

**Lesson 1.2 — What You Need Before You Start**
- Checklist of prerequisites:
  - OpenRouter account + API key (where your AI models come from)
  - Resend account + API key (for email/auth)
  - Railway account (free tier works to start)
  - A business idea or existing business to apply agents to
- How much the AI models cost to run (OpenRouter pricing basics)
- Expected monthly costs breakdown (Railway hosting + AI usage)
- *Deliverable: Student has all accounts created and API keys ready*
- *Video: 4-5 min screen recording of account setup on each platform*

**Lesson 1.3 — Picking Your Template**
- Overview of all 16 templates and who they're for:
  - **Ghost Operator** — Starting a new online business from scratch
  - **TikTok Shop Operator** — Running a TikTok Shop with full automation
  - **Business Builder** — Flexible option for any business type
  - **Service Business** — Coaches, consultants, freelancers
  - **E-Commerce** — Online store owners
  - **Content & Media** — Creators, newsletters, podcasters
  - **Agency / Studio** — Marketing/design agencies
  - **High-Ticket Coaching** — Premium coaching/masterminds
  - **Skool Community** — Community and course creators
  - **Real Estate** — Agents and teams
  - **Local Service Business** — Trades, cleaners, landscapers
  - **SaaS / Software** — Software founders
  - **Social Media Management** — Social media agencies
- Decision framework: "If you do X, pick Y"
- Can you switch later? Yes — how templates work under the hood
- *Deliverable: Student has chosen their template*
- *Video: 6-8 min walkthrough of each template with use case examples*

### MODULE 2: Deploy in 5 Minutes (3 Lessons)

**Goal:** Student has a live, running Ghost ProtoClaw instance they can log into.

**Lesson 2.1 — One-Click Railway Deploy**
- Step-by-step Railway deployment:
  1. Click the deploy link
  2. Fill in the 5 required fields (with screenshots of each)
  3. What each field does and why it matters
  4. Hit deploy and what happens behind the scenes
- What the 6 services are (Ghost ProtoClaw, OpenClaw, Hermes, Codex, Claude Code, PostgreSQL)
- How to find your app URL once deployed
- *Deliverable: Student has a deployed instance on Railway*
- *Video: 5 min real-time deploy recording*

**Lesson 2.2 — Your First Login & Dashboard Tour**
- Logging in with your admin credentials
- Dashboard layout walkthrough:
  - Businesses section
  - Agents section
  - Workflows section
  - Knowledge base
  - Chat (agent testing)
  - Settings
  - Activity logs
  - System health
- Understanding what each section controls
- *Deliverable: Student is logged in and oriented*
- *Video: 8-10 min guided dashboard tour*

**Lesson 2.3 — Troubleshooting Your Deploy**
- Common issues and fixes:
  - "Build failed" — checking Railway logs
  - Services not starting — restart procedures
  - Can't log in — verifying credentials
  - API key errors — checking OpenRouter/Resend setup
  - Database connection issues
- How to check system health in the dashboard
- When to ask for help (community, support channels)
- *Deliverable: Student can self-diagnose common issues*
- *Video: 5-6 min troubleshooting walkthrough*

  - Daily SPS Watch & Tier Alert
  - Weekly Shop Health Report
  - Affiliate Marketplace Outreach Campaign (Open + Targeted)

- What each workflow does, step by step
- *Deliverable: Student knows what each workflow does*
- *Video: 8-10 min walkthrough of key workflows*

**Lesson 4.3 — Customizing & Creating Workflows**
- Editing an existing workflow:
  - Changing the schedule (daily → weekly, etc.)
  - Adjusting which agents are involved
  - Modifying the workflow steps
- Creating a simple custom workflow from scratch
- Best practices: start simple, add complexity later
- *Deliverable: Student has customized at least one workflow*
- *Video: 8-10 min hands-on customization*

**Lesson 4.4 — Managing Approvals & Oversight**
- The Approvals dashboard explained
