# Ghost ProtoClaw — Landing Page Video Script

## Direct Response Edition v2 — "The Money-Maker" Positioning

**Total Runtime: 3:15 - 3:45**
**Primary Target:** Non-techy people who want to make money online with AI agents. They've watched the YouTube videos. They've tried OpenClaw. They got stuck.
**Secondary Target:** Existing business owners who want to automate.

---

## THE CORE INSIGHT

There's a massive audience right now watching YouTube creators show off AI agents building businesses and making money. They want in. They've tried OpenClaw. They've tried setting up agents themselves. They hit a wall — code, APIs, configs, broken integrations — and gave up.

Your product is the FIRST product that gives them the dream without the tech barrier. This video needs to hit them where they live: frustration at being locked out of something they KNOW is the future.

---

## SECTION 1: The Scroll-Stopper (10-15 sec)

**Setup:** You on camera. Hard eye contact. Urgent, direct energy.

**Talking Points:**

> "You've seen the YouTube videos. AI agents building entire online businesses on autopilot. Guys making fifty grand a month while they sleep."
>
> "You wanted in. You tried OpenClaw. You tried setting it up yourself. It kicked your ass."
>
> "This video is why none of that was your fault — and exactly how to fix it today."

**Why this works:** You're speaking directly to the specific pain your target market is experiencing RIGHT NOW. They're watching this video FROM a YouTube video about AI agents. You just validated their whole experience in 15 seconds.

**Editing Note:** Open with zero intro. Cut straight to your face. First frame is your eyes.

---

## SECTION 2: The Enemy — Name The Real Problem (30-40 sec)

**Setup:** Still on camera. Slightly slower. You're giving them permission to stop blaming themselves.

**Talking Points:**

> "Here's what nobody tells you in those YouTube videos. The person setting up those AI agents? They're a developer. Or they've got one on speed dial. Or they spent forty hours watching tutorials until they finally got one thing working."
>
> "OpenClaw is incredible technology. But it was never built for regular people. You have to install it, configure it, wire up APIs, set up databases, deploy to servers, debug when it breaks — and it breaks constantly."
>
> "So you either stay stuck typing prompts into ChatGPT, or you pay a developer five thousand bucks to build you something — and you STILL don't know how to use it."
>
> "That's not your fault. The tech was just too techy. Until now."

**Why this works:** Kennedy's "call out the enemy" plus absolving them of failure. They feel seen. They feel like it wasn't them — it was the tools. Now they're ready to hear your solution.

**Editing Note:** Cut in quick B-roll — someone stressed at a laptop, error messages on screen, a confusing terminal window, complicated API documentation. Text overlay: "It wasn't you. It was the tools."

---

## SECTION 3: The Mechanism (20-25 sec)

**Setup:** Back on camera. Energy lifts. You're the answer to their prayer.

**Talking Points:**

> "That's exactly why I built Ghost ProtoClaw."
>
> "It's OpenClaw, pre-installed, pre-configured, pre-connected, and ready to run. No code. No APIs to wire up. No tutorials. One click deploys the entire system in five to ten minutes — and you're LIVE."
>
> "Not a chatbot. Not a prompt template. A complete AI operating system with real agents that actually build and run businesses."

**Why this works:** You're giving OpenClaw lovers permission to use the tech they already believe in — without the setup that broke them. You're not a competitor to OpenClaw, you're the easy button for it.

**Editing Note:** Text overlay: "OpenClaw. Pre-installed. Pre-configured. Ready to run." Cut to the Railway deploy screen right after "you're live."

---

## SECTION 4: The 5-Minute Deploy (25-30 sec)

**Setup:** Screen recording of deploy. Timer on screen.

> "Watch this."

**[Show: Railway deploy page, 5 fields]**

> "Five fields. That's it. Your email. A password. Two API keys — I walk you through getting them in under 60 seconds each. Hit deploy."

**[Show: Deploy clicked → sped-up build footage with timer]**

> "Ghost ProtoClaw spins up six services automatically. Your AI engine. Your database. Your task runners. Your agents. All connected. Zero configuration on your end."

**[Show: Active status, login screen appears]**

> "Five to ten minutes later — you're logged into your own mission control running a full AI team. This is what OpenClaw was supposed to feel like."

**Why this works:** The timer overlay is proof. You're showing, not telling. And that last line — "this is what OpenClaw was supposed to feel like" — is a dagger. It says to every OpenClaw user: "I get it, I fixed it."

**Editing Note:** Big timer overlay. Stop it at the login screen appearing. Text overlay: "10:00 — Deployed."

>

>
> "Your CFO tracks every single dollar coming in. Tells you exactly what's making money and what to kill."
>

**[Show: TikTok Shop Operator dashboard with all 11 agents]**

> "Or if you want to go the TikTok Shop route — the TikTok Shop Operator. Eleven specialized agents running your entire shop."
>
> "Product research and listing-quality scoring. Faceless content creation — three to five videos per day, per product. AI UGC at volume. Open Plan and Targeted Plan affiliate outreach through TikTok's Affiliate Marketplace. GMV Max ad campaigns and Spark Ads. Late-dispatch firefighting so your Shop Performance Score keeps climbing. Customer service through TikTok IM. Compliance — INFORM Act, AI labels, the whole 2026 stack. Settlement-driven unit economics straight from TikTok's Settlement Reports. It's the whole operation."

**Why this works:** You're stacking THREE value propositions on one product. Want to make money? Ghost Operator. Want TikTok Shop money? TikTok Shop Operator. Already have a business? Fourteen more templates or custom build. Every viewer fits one of these buckets.

**Editing Note:** Each agent role in Ghost Operator gets its own text overlay that snaps on and off. Same for the TikTok Shop Operator. Pacing should pick up — punchy cuts.

## SECTION 6: The Dream Outcome (25-30 sec)

**Setup:** Back on camera. Slow down. Paint the picture.

**Talking Points:**

> "Here's what your Tuesday looks like thirty days from now."
>
> "You wake up. Your revenue report is already in your inbox — your CFO wrote it while you slept. Your new TikTok videos posted this morning — your content creator made them. Your sales went up overnight — your affiliate manager landed three new creators. Your customer messages? All answered, all within four hours. Your CEO has already scouted three new product opportunities for you to review with your morning coffee."
>
> "You didn't type a prompt. You didn't fight a config file. You didn't chase a single task."
>
> "Your AI team ran your business while you lived your life."

**Why this works:** Hormozi's "sell the vacation, not the plane ticket" at max volume. This is the moment they see themselves living the dream they've been watching on YouTube.

**Editing Note:** Simple lifestyle B-roll. Coffee, phone screen showing dashboard, someone smiling, maybe a walk or beach shot if you have it. Keep it grounded — not rich-guy fantasy, just "calm morning."

**Setup:** Back on camera. Confident. The close.

**Talking Points:**

> "Let's talk price, because this part's crazy."
>
> "Hiring real people to do what Ghost ProtoClaw does would cost you ten grand a month. Minimum. Hiring a developer to build a custom AI system would run you fifty grand easy — and you'd STILL be learning how to use it."
>
> "Ghost ProtoClaw is one hundred forty-nine dollars. One time."
>
> "You get the full app. All thirteen templates. Every agent. Every workflow. Every integration. Deployed in under ten minutes. Yours forever."
>
> "One payment. Your data. Your agents. Your business."

**Why this works:** Kennedy's price anchoring. The $10K and $50K frames make $149 feel like a mispriced product that's about to go up.

**Editing Note:** Price anchor overlays:
- "$10,000/month" (crossed out)
- "$50,000 custom build" (crossed out)  
- "$149 — ONE TIME" (highlighted, bold, large)

**Setup:** Final on-camera shot. Direct and firm.

**Talking Points:**

> "Every day you wait is another day your AI team isn't running your business."
>
> "Another day of typing prompts. Another day of watching other people do what you could be doing."
>
> "Click below. Deploy in ten minutes. Your AI team is live before you finish your coffee."
>
> "Stop watching the YouTube videos. Start being the next one."

**Why this works:** That final line is a mic drop. It flips their identity from spectator to operator. Kennedy would be proud.

**Editing Note:** Hold 2 seconds on your face after the last line. Cut to end card:
