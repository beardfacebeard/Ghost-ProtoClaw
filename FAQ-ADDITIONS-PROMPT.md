## New Section: "Don't Have a Business Yet? Start Here"

**Q: I don't have a business yet — can Ghost ProtoClaw still help me?**
A: That's actually one of the best times to start using it. The Ghost Operator template was built specifically for aspiring entrepreneurs who have an idea (or don't even have one yet) and want AI to help them research, plan, build, and grow a real online business. You don't need experience, a huge budget, or technical skills. You just need the drive to get started — your AI team handles the heavy lifting while you make the final calls.

**Q: What is the Ghost Operator?**
A: It's a complete AI-powered business launch system built into Ghost ProtoClaw. When you select the Ghost Operator template, you get a team of 5 specialized AI agents that work together like a startup team:

Your **Scout (CEO)** researches online business opportunities, scores them based on your goals, available time, budget, and skills, and presents ranked options with realistic revenue timelines — including the downsides. Your **Strategist (COO)** takes your chosen opportunity and builds a concrete phased plan with 90-day milestones and resource requirements. Your **Builder (CTO)** executes the build tasks one at a time, documents everything, and tests before anything goes live. Your **Growth agent (CMO)** markets your business using free organic channels first — TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest, LinkedIn, cold email — runs weekly experiments, doubles down on winners, and permanently retires approaches that fail twice. And your **Revenue Monitor (CFO)** tracks daily revenue, identifies which channel and offer drove it, and surfaces the single clearest next action to grow your income.

They operate in phases — research, decide, build, grow, optimize — and learn from every cycle. Nothing gets published, nothing gets spent, and no irreversible actions happen without your explicit approval.

**Q: What if I only have a few hours a week and a small budget?**
A: Ghost Operator was designed exactly for that. When you set up your workspace, you tell your agents how many hours per week you can commit, your starting budget, your skills, and your risk tolerance. Every recommendation and plan they create is tailored to those constraints. If you have 5 hours a week and $50 to start, they won't suggest strategies that require 40 hours and $5,000. The system meets you where you are and scales with you as you grow.

An optional Organic Ladder addon layers in an off-platform digital product funnel (Stripe + Resend) for operators who also want to run a creator-economy layer alongside their Shop.

**Q: Do I need any money to start with the Ghost Operator or TikTok Shop Operator?**

The TikTok Shop Operator will require some product investment and potential ad spend as you scale, but it starts with organic content first. Your AI Finance agent enforces strict unit economics so you always know your margins before spending more. ## New Section: "For Aspiring TikTok Shop Sellers"

**Q: Do I need to show my face on camera?**
A: No. The entire TikTok Shop Operator system is built around "faceless" content formats. Your AI team creates hands-only product demos, AI voiceover videos with b-roll, text-overlay storytelling with trending audio, ASMR unboxing content, before-and-after transformations, and AI avatar talking head videos using tools like HeyGen. You build a consistent brand identity with a digital spokesperson — no personal filming required.

**Q: What are the realistic costs to launch a TikTok Shop?**
A: There are a few layers of cost to be aware of. You'll need Ghost ProtoClaw hosting (a few dollars per month on Railway), AI model usage for your 11 agents (varies based on activity), Social Media Hub via Late (~$33/mo) or Ayrshare (~$149/mo) so the agents can publish to TikTok and pull post analytics, initial product inventory from suppliers, and optionally AI content generation tools like HeyGen (~$24/mo) or Creatify (~$39/mo). The system starts you with organic content before paid ads. Your Finance & Settlement Analyst agent tracks every dollar straight from TikTok Settlement Reports and enforces a minimum contribution margin of 20% per product — meaning you always know if a product is profitable before scaling spend. TikTok takes a 6% referral fee on most categories (some at 5%; new sellers get a 3% promotional rate for the first 30 days post-first-sale). Be prepared for some upfront investment in inventory with no guarantee of return — that's normal for any product-based business.

**Q: How does the system keep me compliant with TikTok's rules?**
A: You have a dedicated Compliance Officer agent whose only job is making sure everything follows TikTok Shop policies, the INFORM Act (with annual re-verification reminders), FTC guidelines, the 2026 AI content labeling requirements (TikTok integrated C2PA Content Credentials in January 2025; promotional AI avatar / AI voice content needs both an AI label AND a paid-partnership label), music licensing (TikTok Commercial Music Library only in shoppable content), and the brutal 180-day balance withhold rule for counterfeit claims (extended from 90 days in October 2025). It reviews content before publishing, flags potential issues, and ensures your store stays in good standing. This is critical because a single counterfeit / IP claim or INFORM Act lapse can suspend your shop and freeze settlements for six months.

**Q: What if a product doesn't sell?**
A: The system has a built-in "fail fast" philosophy. Your Product Analyst validates unit economics before you commit to inventory. Your Growth Strategist tests content variations and permanently retires approaches that fail twice. Your Finance Analyst monitors margins in real-time. If an ad campaign pushes your contribution margin below 15%, it gets paused immediately. The whole system is designed to find what works, cut what doesn't, and reallocate resources to winners — not to throw good money after bad.

## New Section: "Updates & Staying Current"

**Q: What happens when new features or templates are added to Ghost ProtoClaw?**
A: When we ship updates to Ghost ProtoClaw — whether that's new agent templates, improved workflows, additional integrations, enhanced dashboard features, or security improvements — those updates are pushed to the platform. When you redeploy or update your instance, you automatically get access to the latest features, templates, and improvements. You don't need to manually install anything or rebuild from scratch.

**Q: Will my existing agents and data be affected by updates?**
A: No. Updates add new capabilities and fix issues, but they don't overwrite your existing agents, workflows, knowledge base, or settings. Your custom configurations stay exactly as you set them up. Think of it like your phone getting a software update — you get new features, but your apps, photos, and settings stay the same.

**Q: How do I know when something new is available?**
A: The platform includes a built-in changelog and help center that documents what's new. When significant features are added — like a new operator template or a new integration — you'll see it reflected in the template library and integration options on your dashboard. We also document updates so you can see exactly what changed and decide if you want to take advantage of new features.

**Q: Do I need to be technical to apply updates?**
A: Not at all. If you're hosted on Railway (which most users are), updates can be applied with a simple redeploy — essentially one click. The platform handles database migrations, dependency updates, and configuration changes automatically during the deploy process. You don't need to touch any code.
