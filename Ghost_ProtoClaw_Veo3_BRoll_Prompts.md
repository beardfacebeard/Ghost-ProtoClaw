# Ghost ProtoClaw — Veo 3 B-Roll Prompts

## How to Use These

Each prompt is written for Veo 3's text-to-video generation. Copy and paste directly. Each clip should be 4-8 seconds — that's all you need for B-roll cuts in short-form and the landing page video.

Generate each one, review, re-prompt if needed. Save the best takes into a "B-Roll Library" folder in CapCut so you can drag and drop them into any video.

## CATEGORY 1: The Frustration / "Before" Shots

These go with Sections 1-2 of the landing page and Angles 1, 2, 8 of the short-form scripts. They show the pain of trying to use AI the hard way.

### Prompt 1 — Frustrated at Laptop

> Cinematic close-up of a person sitting at a desk at night, illuminated by the blue glow of a laptop screen. They rub their temples with both hands and exhale slowly, visibly frustrated. The laptop screen shows blurry lines of code and terminal text. Shallow depth of field. Moody, warm desk lamp in the background. Shot on a 50mm lens. 4K. Realistic.

### Prompt 2 — Confused by Code

> Over-the-shoulder shot of a person staring at a laptop screen filled with dense code, terminal commands, and error messages in red text. Their hand hovers over the keyboard but doesn't type. The room is dimly lit with a single desk lamp. The person slowly shakes their head. Cinematic. Realistic lighting. 4K.

### Prompt 3 — Giving Up and Closing the Laptop

> Medium shot of a person in a home office slowly closing their laptop lid with one hand, leaning back in their chair, and staring at the ceiling. The room is lit by a warm desk lamp. A coffee mug sits on the desk, half empty. The mood is defeat and exhaustion. Cinematic color grading. Slow motion. 4K.

### Prompt 4 — Error Messages Montage

> Close-up of a laptop screen showing a cascade of red error messages and failed build logs in a dark terminal window. The text scrolls rapidly. A cursor blinks at the bottom. No person visible. The screen reflects faintly on the desk surface. Dark, moody lighting. Cinematic. 4K.

### Prompt 5 — Typing Prompts Into a Chat Window Endlessly

> Close-up of hands typing rapidly on a keyboard. A chat interface is visible on the laptop screen showing a long back-and-forth conversation with an AI chatbot. The person types a message, waits, reads the response, then starts typing again. The cycle feels repetitive and endless. Soft overhead lighting. Realistic. 4K.

## CATEGORY 2: The "After" / Dream Outcome Shots

These go with Section 6 of the landing page and Angles 3, 7, 12 of the short-form scripts. They show the calm, successful, automated life.

### Prompt 6 — Morning Coffee Check

> Cinematic shot of a person sitting at a kitchen table in the early morning. Warm golden sunlight streams through a window. They hold a white coffee mug in one hand and casually scroll their phone with the other, smiling slightly. The mood is calm, relaxed, and successful. Shallow depth of field. Shot like an Apple commercial. 4K.

### Prompt 7 — Checking Phone in Bed

> Close-up of a person lying in bed in the early morning, picking up their phone from the nightstand. Soft warm light from the window. They look at the screen and smile with a subtle, satisfied expression. The room is clean and minimal. Cozy but aspirational. Cinematic. 4K.

### Prompt 8 — Walking Outside With Confidence

> Tracking shot of a person walking down a tree-lined sidewalk on a sunny morning, wearing casual clothes, earbuds in, looking relaxed and confident. They glance at their phone briefly, smile, and keep walking. The background is slightly blurred with golden sunlight filtering through leaves. Cinematic slow motion. 4K.

### Prompt 9 — Working From a Coffee Shop (Relaxed)

> Medium shot of a person sitting in a bright, modern coffee shop with a laptop open in front of them. They take a sip of coffee, glance at the screen, and nod approvingly. The vibe is effortless and relaxed, not stressed or grinding. Natural light from large windows. Other people blurred in the background. Cinematic. 4K.

### Prompt 10 — Closing the Laptop and Leaving

> Medium shot of a person in a clean home office gently closing their laptop with a satisfied expression. They stand up, grab their keys from the desk, and walk out of frame. The desk is neat and organized. Sunlight fills the room. The mood is "done for the day at 10am." Cinematic. 4K.

## CATEGORY 3: Technology / Futuristic / AI Vibes

These are accent B-roll for transitions, text overlay backgrounds, and energy moments throughout all videos.

### Prompt 11 — Neural Network / AI Visualization

> Abstract cinematic visualization of a glowing neural network. Bright blue and white nodes connected by pulsing light beams, floating in a dark space. The network expands and connects new nodes dynamically. The camera slowly pushes through the network. Dark background. Futuristic. Clean. 4K.

### Prompt 12 — Data Flow / Digital Dashboard

> Cinematic close-up of a futuristic transparent digital dashboard floating in mid-air. Graphs, charts, and data streams glow in soft blue and white. Numbers tick upward. The camera slowly orbits around the dashboard. Dark background with subtle ambient light. Sleek and modern. 4K.

### Prompt 13 — Robot Hands Typing

> Close-up of sleek, modern robotic hands typing on a glowing keyboard. The fingers move precisely and rapidly. Soft blue LED light illuminates the metallic surface of the hands. The background is dark and minimal. Cinematic. Futuristic but grounded. 4K.

### Prompt 14 — Server Room / Deployment

> Cinematic tracking shot moving down a row of glowing server racks in a modern data center. LED lights blink green and blue along the racks. Cable bundles run neatly between servers. Cool blue ambient lighting. Slight fog or haze in the air. The mood is power and scale. 4K.

### Prompt 15 — One Button Press

> Extreme close-up of a finger pressing a single glowing blue button on a dark, minimal surface. The button illuminates brighter on press. A subtle ripple of light radiates outward from the button across the surface. Dark background. Cinematic. Slow motion. 4K.

## CATEGORY 4: Money / Revenue / Success

These go with Angles 3, 6, 9, 12 and the price anchoring sections.

### Prompt 16 — Revenue Numbers Going Up

> Close-up of a laptop screen showing a clean, modern analytics dashboard. A revenue line chart animates upward with a smooth green line. Dollar amounts tick up in real time in the corner of the screen. The screen glows against a dark desk. No person visible. Cinematic. 4K.

### Prompt 17 — Phone Notification of a Sale

> Close-up of a smartphone lying on a wooden desk. The screen lights up with a notification that reads "New Sale — $47.00." A second later, another notification appears: "New Sale — $89.00." The phone buzzes subtly with each notification. Warm ambient lighting. Shallow depth of field. Cinematic. 4K.

### Prompt 18 — Cash Register / Ka-Ching Moment

> Stylized close-up of a vintage brass cash register drawer sliding open in slow motion, revealing neatly stacked bills inside. Warm golden light. Shallow depth of field. The background is dark and out of focus. Cinematic. Aspirational but not flashy. 4K.

## CATEGORY 5: The "Team" / Agents Working

These are conceptual shots representing AI agents doing work. Good for Sections 5-6 of the landing page and Angles 4, 7.

### Prompt 19 — Five Silhouettes in a Board Room

> Wide cinematic shot of a modern glass boardroom at night. Five silhouettes sit around a sleek conference table, each illuminated by the glow of a laptop screen in front of them. The city skyline is visible through floor-to-ceiling windows behind them. Cool blue and warm amber lighting. The mood is a team working late on something important. 4K.

### Prompt 20 — Assembly Line of Screens

> Cinematic tracking shot moving past a row of five computer monitors, each showing a different task: one has a marketing dashboard, one has a financial spreadsheet, one has a content calendar, one has a chat interface, one has analytics graphs. Each screen glows softly. No people. The camera glides past smoothly. Dark room. Modern. 4K.

### Prompt 21 — AI Agent Avatar Coming to Life

> Close-up of a sleek, minimal humanoid avatar on a dark screen. The avatar's eyes illuminate with a soft blue glow as it activates. Digital particles swirl around it. The avatar tilts its head slightly as if listening. The mood is intelligent and helpful, not threatening. Dark background. Cinematic. 4K.

## CATEGORY 6: TikTok Shop Specific

These go with TikTok Shop Operator content — Angles 4, 5, and anywhere you talk about the TikTok template.

### Prompt 22 — Products Being Packaged for Shipping

> Cinematic overhead shot of hands carefully placing a small product into a branded shipping box on a clean white table. Packing peanuts and tissue paper visible. The hands close the box and place a shipping label on top. Bright, clean lighting. The mood is organized and efficient. 4K.

### Prompt 23 — Phone Scrolling TikTok Shop

> Close-up of a hand holding a smartphone, scrolling through a TikTok Shop feed. Product videos play as the thumb swipes up. The phone screen is bright and colorful against a blurred background. Natural lighting. Realistic. 4K.

### Prompt 24 — Content Creation Setup (Faceless)

> Overhead shot of a clean white desk with a ring light, a smartphone on a tripod, and a product placed in the center. A pair of hands arranges the product for filming. The setup is minimal and aesthetic. Bright, even lighting. The mood is a creator's workspace. 4K.
