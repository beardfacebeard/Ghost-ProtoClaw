## BUSINESS FIELDS (Step 2: Business Details)

### Business Name
```
Ghost ProtoClaw
```

### Summary
```
Ghost ProtoClaw is an AI-powered platform that lets non-techy business owners deploy a full team of AI agents to run their business — without writing a single line of code. It provides 13+ ready-to-deploy business templates, automated workflows, knowledge bases, and a mission control dashboard. The product is sold as a digital product with optional managed hosting at ghostprotoclaw.com, targeting builders who want to automate real business operations with AI.
```

### Brand Voice
```
Direct, honest, and builder-to-builder. Speak like someone who has been in the trenches — no corporate fluff, no fake urgency, no hype. Lead with what the product actually does, show real capability, and respect the reader's intelligence. Use plain English. Be confident without being salesy. The tone should feel like a smart friend explaining a powerful tool over coffee.
```

### Main Goals
```
Drive consistent sales of Ghost ProtoClaw across all three tiers (App Only $149, Founding Setup $497, Founding Concierge $997). Build a waitlist and community of non-techy builders. Create content that demonstrates real product capability and earns trust. Convert free social traffic into paying customers. Grow hosted subscriptions ($79/month) for recurring revenue.
```

### Core Offers
```
App Only — $149 one-time: Self-hosted Ghost ProtoClaw with full documentation, community access, and self-service deploy kit. Founding Setup — $497 one-time: Guided onboarding with a 1:1 setup call, configuration assistance, and 30-day priority email support. Founding Concierge — $997 one-time: White-glove onboarding with a dedicated success manager, priority support queue, and custom agent configuration. Hosted with Us — $79/month: Fully managed Railway hosting with automatic updates, monitoring, and 99.5% uptime SLA.
```

### Offer and Audience Notes
```
TARGET AUDIENCE: Non-techy business owners and solo operators who are drowning in manual work — content creation, customer follow-up, scheduling, reporting — and know AI can help but have no idea how to set it up. They have tried ChatGPT or other AI tools and found them useful for one-off tasks but frustrating as a real business solution. They want something that actually runs their operations, not just answers questions. Most are earning revenue already (or close to it) and are willing to invest $149-$997 in a tool that gives them back hours every week. They are action-takers who value honesty, practical demonstrations, and plain English over hype and jargon.

SECONDARY AUDIENCE: Tech-curious entrepreneurs who understand the value of AI agents and want a production-ready platform without building from scratch. They appreciate the open-source foundation (OpenClaw) but want the managed, template-driven experience Ghost ProtoClaw provides.

HOW THE OFFER WORKS: Ghost ProtoClaw is sold as a one-time digital product purchase with an optional recurring hosting add-on. The buyer selects a tier based on how much hands-on help they want:

- App Only ($149) — self-hosted, self-service. The buyer gets the full application, documentation, all 13+ templates, and community access. They handle their own hosting on Railway or similar. Best for buyers with some technical comfort or access to a developer.

- Founding Setup ($497) — guided onboarding. Everything in App Only plus a 1:1 setup call, configuration help, and 30 days of priority email support. Best for buyers who want a safety net during their first month.

- Founding Concierge ($997) — white-glove. Everything in Founding Setup plus a dedicated success manager, priority support queue, custom agent configuration for their specific business, and 60-day proactive check-ins. Best for buyers who want everything done for them.

- Hosted With Us ($79/month) — add-on to any tier. Fully managed Railway hosting with automatic updates, monitoring, and 99.5% uptime SLA. Best for anyone who does not want to manage a server.

POSITIONING: Ghost ProtoClaw is not another chatbot or ChatGPT wrapper. It is a full AI operating system — multiple specialized agents working as a team, automated workflows, a knowledge base that learns your business, and a mission control dashboard. The closest analogy is hiring an entire operations team for a one-time cost. The product competes on depth (full agent team vs. single bot), ease of use (no code, template-driven), and ownership (self-hosted option, open-source foundation).

BUYING PSYCHOLOGY: The primary emotional driver is relief — these buyers are overwhelmed and want to feel like they finally have a team backing them up. The secondary driver is competitiveness — they see other businesses using AI and don't want to fall behind. Never exploit fear or make income promises. Lead with capability demonstrations and let the product sell itself.

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. No refunds. This must be communicated before any purchase. The no-refund policy is justified by full source code access and tier-appropriate support.
```

The product provides tools and automation — results depend on how the customer uses them. Never offer unauthorized discounts, custom pricing, or payment plans that have not been explicitly approved. REFUND POLICY: All sales are final — no refunds. This must be communicated clearly before any purchase. Never promise or imply refunds are available. COMPETITOR CONDUCT: Never disparage competitors by name. Focus on what Ghost ProtoClaw does well, not what others do poorly. PLATFORM COMPLIANCE: Follow every platform's terms of service strictly. Never use bots, bulk automation, or identical mass messages. CUSTOMER DATA: Treat all prospect and customer information as confidential. ESCALATION REQUIRED: Escalate immediately for refund requests, chargeback threats, negative public reviews, legal questions, partnership proposals, and any request to modify pricing or terms.

### Banned Claims
```
Never guarantee specific income, revenue, or business results from using Ghost ProtoClaw. Never claim the product replaces human judgment entirely. Never promise refunds — all sales are final. Never offer unauthorized discounts or custom pricing. Never claim Ghost ProtoClaw is "the only" or "the first" AI agent platform. Never make false comparisons to specific competitors. Never promise features that do not exist yet. Never claim zero technical knowledge is needed for self-hosting (App Only tier requires basic Railway/server setup). ### 6. Platform Safety Rules — All Channels
```
**Category:** policies
```
PLATFORM COMPLIANCE IS NON-NEGOTIABLE. Account safety always overrides growth targets.

LINKEDIN: Max 20-25 connection requests/day for new accounts. Max 100 DMs/week to connections. Comment on prospects' posts before connecting. No bots — LinkedIn actively detects them.

PINTEREST: Max 50 pins/day. Max 100 follows/day. Every pin must link to a working URL. Keyword-rich descriptions. No mass-pinning automation.

YOUTUBE: Consistent upload schedule matters more than frequency. Optimize titles, thumbnails, and descriptions for search. Respond to comments within 24 hours.

COLD EMAIL: Configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC first. Warm up domains starting at 10-20/day. Personalize every email. Max 5 follow-ups spaced 3-5 days. CAN-SPAM compliance required.
```
